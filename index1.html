LocalEats - Your Ultimate Food Delivery Companion

Introducing LocalEats, your go-to food delivery app designed to satisfy your cravings with ease and convenience. Starting with a seamless login process, our app utilizes GPS technology to present you with a curated selection of popular restaurants in your hometown or nearby city.

Explore each restaurant's complete menu offerings at your fingertips, from mouth-watering appetizers to decadent desserts. Once you've made your selection, effortlessly add items to your cart and customize your order to your liking.

But that's not all - LocalEats goes above and beyond by allowing you to send a thoughtful message along with your order, making it the perfect surprise for your loved ones.

When it comes time to pay, enjoy a hassle-free experience with multiple payment options including PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, debit/credit cards, or cash on delivery.

With LocalEats, indulging in your favorite dishes has never been easier.

Note: The application features mentioned above are completely developed in Figma. It is a prototype of the application. There was no Backend involved in it.

Step Tracker App - Stay Active, Stay Healthy

Step Tracker is a mobile app designed to help users track their daily activity, stay motivated, and lead a healthier lifestyle. Drawing inspiration from my mobile's step-tracking system, it allows users to monitor their daily, weekly, and monthly steps.

With customizable goals ranging from 1000 to 20000 steps, users can challenge themselves and strive for improvement.

The app also provides insights into activity trends, allowing users to track their progress over time. Additionally, users can share their achievements on social media platforms to celebrate their milestones with friends and family.

With its user-friendly interface, Step Tracker caters to fitness enthusiasts and anyone looking to adopt a more active lifestyle.

Note: The application features mentioned above are completely developed in Figma. It is a prototype of the application. There was no Backend involved in it.
